## Core Addition to the Skill

Before judging whether a stock is ready for bottom stabilization or trend entry, first identify whether its sector is:

- a repeated main-line sector,
- a low-position relay sector,
- a defensive/geopolitical hedge,
- or only a one-day pulse.

Trend-entry rules should be applied mainly to confirmed main-line or confirmed relay sectors. One-day pulses require extra confirmation.
